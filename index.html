Artists

"Musicians are musicians; they are not business people" is the sentiment we've heard from talking to independent artists here in Berkeley and in Hawai'i. Artists should have the ability to solely focus on their music and not have to worry about booking a gig or making a music video. We want to connect you to freelancing creatives that'll help you produce the content you want without the overhead.

group_add

Freelancers

Whether you're a local restaurant looking to find someone to play live music or a photographer looking to make a few extra bucks, the Rechord platform wants to give you the opportunity to find talented artists in your area.
